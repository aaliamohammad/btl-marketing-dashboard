✅ Approval:  All outreach requires your sign-off before launch. No exceptions.
👁 Branding:  Eye doctors get "The Art of the Eyes"; everyone else gets "The Modern Medicine Symposium."
✍️ Style:  No em dashes. Short paragraphs. Warm peer-level tone. "Bring lunch" not "grab lunch."
