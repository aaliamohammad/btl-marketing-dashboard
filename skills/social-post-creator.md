# Skill: Social Post Creator

## Purpose
Creates engaging social media content for BTL Aesthetics across LinkedIn, Instagram, and Facebook — targeting practice owners, office managers, and industry professionals.

## When to Use
- Regular social content calendar
- Event promotion posts
- Product highlight posts
- Industry insights and thought leadership
- Post-event recaps

## Inputs Required
- **Platform**: LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, or multi-platform
- **Post type**: Event promo, product highlight, industry insight, testimonial, behind-the-scenes, educational
- **Key message**: What's the one thing you want people to take away?
- **CTA**: What should they do? (register, DM, visit link, comment)
- **Visual direction** (optional): What kind of image/graphic to pair with it

## Platform-Specific Formatting

### LinkedIn
- Professional tone, practice-owner focused
- 150-300 words optimal
- Use line breaks for readability
- Include 3-5 relevant hashtags
- End with a question or CTA to drive engagement

### Instagram
- More visual and conversational
- Caption: 100-200 words
- Include 10-15 hashtags (mix of branded and discovery)
- Carousel posts: 5-7 slides with concise text per slide
- Stories: Short, punchy, with poll/question stickers

### Facebook
- Conversational and community-focused
- 100-250 words
- Include link if driving to registration or landing page
- 2-3 hashtags max

## Content Themes
1. **Event Promotion**: Countdown posts, speaker highlights, attendee benefits
2. **Product Spotlight**: Treatment benefits, before/after concepts, ROI for practices
3. **Industry Insights**: Trends in aesthetics, practice growth tips
4. **Social Proof**: Event photos, practice success stories
5. **Educational**: Treatment explainers, device comparisons, patient demand data

## Output Format
```
---
SOCIAL POST
Platform: [platform]
Type: [post type]
---

[Post copy]

Hashtags: [hashtags]

Visual suggestion: [description of recommended image/graphic]

Best posting time: [recommendation]
```

## Quality Checks
- [ ] Platform-appropriate length and tone
- [ ] Clear single CTA
- [ ] Hashtags are relevant and current
- [ ] Visual suggestion aligns with content
- [ ] No em dashes
- [ ] Compliant with medical aesthetics advertising guidelines
