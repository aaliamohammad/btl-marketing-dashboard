# Skill: Blog Writer

## Purpose
Creates SEO-optimized, practice-focused blog content that positions BTL Aesthetics devices as solutions for medical practices looking to expand their offerings.

## When to Use
- Creating educational content for practice owners
- Building thought leadership around aesthetic treatments
- Supporting event marketing with relevant content
- Generating lead magnet supporting content

## Inputs Required
- **Topic or keyword**: What the blog post is about
- **Target audience**: Which practice type(s) this is for
- **Goal**: Education, lead generation, SEO, or event promotion
- **Length**: Short (500-800 words), Medium (800-1200), Long (1200-2000)
- **CTA**: What should the reader do after reading?

## Blog Structure
```
# [Headline — compelling, keyword-rich, under 70 characters]

## Introduction (2-3 paragraphs)
- Hook: Start with a problem or opportunity the reader faces
- Context: Why this matters now
- Promise: What they'll learn

## Body (3-5 sections with H2 headers)
- Each section addresses one key point
- Include data, examples, or case references where possible
- Naturally mention relevant BTL products as solutions (not forced)

## Conclusion
- Summarize key takeaways
- Clear CTA

## SEO Metadata
- Meta title: [under 60 characters]
- Meta description: [under 160 characters]
- Target keyword: [primary keyword]
- Secondary keywords: [2-3 related terms]
```

## Content Guidelines
- Write for practice owners and office managers, not patients
- Focus on business impact (ROI, patient satisfaction, competitive advantage)
- Reference real treatment outcomes and benefits
- Avoid overly clinical language — keep it accessible
- Naturally integrate BTL product mentions without being salesy
- Include practical takeaways the reader can act on

## Tone
- Knowledgeable but approachable
- Peer-to-peer (practice professional to practice professional)
- Forward-looking and optimistic about the industry
- NOT marketing copy — educational and genuinely helpful

## Quality Checks
- [ ] Headline is compelling and keyword-optimized
- [ ] Introduction hooks the reader within first 2 sentences
- [ ] Each section has a clear purpose
- [ ] BTL product mentions feel natural, not forced
- [ ] CTA is clear and relevant
- [ ] SEO metadata is complete
- [ ] No em dashes in the body
