# BTL Aesthetics — Brand Context

## Company Overview
BTL Aesthetics is a global leader in medical aesthetic devices. The company develops non-invasive and minimally invasive technologies that help medical practices offer cutting-edge body contouring, skin rejuvenation, and wellness treatments.

## Product Portfolio

### EMSCULPT NEO
- **What it does**: Simultaneously burns fat and builds muscle using HIFEM+ and radiofrequency
- **Best for**: Body contouring, core strengthening, athletic performance
- **Practice appeal**: High patient demand, strong ROI, treats multiple areas

### EMFACE
- **What it does**: Non-invasive facial treatment using synchronized RF and HIFES technology
- **Best for**: Facial lifting and toning without needles or surgery
- **Practice appeal**: Growing demand for non-surgical face treatments, quick treatment time

### EMSELLA
- **What it does**: Non-invasive pelvic floor strengthening using HIFEM technology
- **Best for**: Urinary incontinence, pelvic floor health, intimate wellness
- **Practice appeal**: Underserved market, high patient retention, treats both men and women

### EMTONE
- **What it does**: Simultaneously delivers RF and targeted pressure energy for cellulite treatment
- **Best for**: Cellulite reduction, skin texture improvement
- **Practice appeal**: Addresses one of the most requested aesthetic concerns

### EXION
- **What it does**: Monopolar RF and targeted ultrasound for skin rejuvenation
- **Best for**: Skin tightening, wrinkle reduction, facial rejuvenation
- **Practice appeal**: Versatile platform with multiple treatment heads

### EMVITAL
- **What it does**: Advanced body treatment technology
- **Practice appeal**: Newest addition to the BTL portfolio

### EXOMIND
- **What it does**: Brain stimulation technology
- **Practice appeal**: Expanding BTL's reach beyond aesthetics into wellness/neurological

### STARFORMER
- **What it does**: Pelvic floor and core muscle treatment platform
- **Practice appeal**: Broad treatment applications, scalable for high volume

## Territory
- **Primary**: Oklahoma and North Texas
- **Key metros**: Oklahoma City, Tulsa, Dallas-Fort Worth
- **Practice types**: Aesthetics, wellness, dermatology, OB/GYN, dental, medical spas

## Brand Voice Summary
- Professional but warm
- Peer-to-peer (we're colleagues in the industry, not a vendor pushing product)
- Solution-oriented (lead with what the practice gains)
- Evidence-based (reference clinical results and patient outcomes)
- Respectful of the practitioner's expertise

## Key Differentiators
- Only company with simultaneous RF + HIFEM technology
- Extensive clinical research and publications
- Comprehensive device portfolio covering face, body, and pelvic floor
- Strong training and support infrastructure
- Growing consumer awareness driving patient demand
